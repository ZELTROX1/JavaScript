Frequently Asked Questions

1. What are your business hours?
Our business hours are Monday through Friday, 9:00 AM to 6:00 PM EST. We are closed on weekends and major holidays.

2. What is your refund policy?
We offer a 30-day money-back guarantee on all products. If you're not satisfied with your purchase, you can return it within 30 days for a full refund. The item must be in original condition and packaging.

3. How can I contact customer support?
You can reach our customer support team through:
- Email: support@company.com
- Phone: 1-800-123-4567
- Live chat on our website (available during business hours)

4. Do you offer international shipping?
Yes, we ship internationally to most countries. Shipping costs and delivery times vary by location. Please check our shipping page for detailed information about your specific country.

5. What payment methods do you accept?
We accept the following payment methods:
- Credit cards (Visa, MasterCard, American Express)
- PayPal
- Apple Pay
- Google Pay
- Bank transfers (for orders over $500)

6. How long does shipping take?
Domestic shipping typically takes 3-5 business days. International shipping can take 7-14 business days depending on the destination and customs processing times.

7. Can I track my order?
Yes, once your order ships, you'll receive a tracking number via email. You can use this number to track your package on our website or the carrier's website.

8. Do you offer warranties on your products?
Most of our products come with a manufacturer's warranty. The warranty period varies by product, typically ranging from 1-3 years. Check the product description for specific warranty information.

9. How do I create an account?
You can create an account by clicking the "Sign Up" button on our homepage. You'll need to provide your email address and create a password. Account creation is free and gives you access to order tracking and faster checkout.

10. What if I receive a damaged item?
If you receive a damaged item, please contact us immediately at support@company.com with photos of the damage. We'll arrange for a replacement or refund as appropriate.

This document serves as a comprehensive guide to our most frequently asked questions. For additional inquiries not covered here, please don't hesitate to contact our customer support team.
